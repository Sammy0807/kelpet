How I Met Him

It was my last semester at the University of Houston-Clear Lake Campus in May 2015. I always saw Peter around campus at school, most often, while studying in one of the computer labs, which was my favourite place to get my school work done. He would stare at me, this made me feel awkward, but i felt he was just drawn to me. I didn't think much of it because i was mostly really focused on my studies. Weeks passed, and i saw a friend request from him on facebook, I immediately rejected it, because i normally dont accept request from people i did'nt know. From then on, i saw more of him because we lived in the same student apartment complex, we also had alot of mutual friends, this made us converse briefly or exchange pleasantries each time we came across each other.

Excitingly, the following semester in December of 2015, a number of our friends graduated and threw an awesome party. Peter was there, and he came up to me to strike a conversation. He really captured my attention the whole night, So we exchanged contact information (Thats the magical day that started it all). I was so drawn to him, the more we talked, the more we spent time together, the more i enjoyed his company and the more i saw of him. He warmed into my heart and is my best friend, the love of my life, I can't wait to spend the rest of my life with this man!!! <3

Peter

They say when love is real, it never fades, It just keeps getting stronger with time. That is the way I feel with you. Married life will be more fun with you as my Otsanya. I will never give you away for anything, you are so special to me. I love you, Always and Forever!

How I Met Her

It was the fall of 2015 and finals were coming up, so i spent alot of time in the computer lab studying. I was doing pretty decent with my grades, so from time to time i will lean over to chat with my classmates in the lab. Then it happened, you know how you are in a room and it is a bit noisy, then there is a sudden silence... the prettiest girl i had ever seen in my life walked in. I was weak to my knees and just stared. Everyone in the room was also looking at her, but i noticed she was looking back at me too. I wanted to go say hello, but she seemed so focused on what she was doing. I decided to let fate determine our union.

When the semester was over, a mutual friend was having a graduation party, so I went there with a friend and since I knew she was going to be there, I did a little facebook recon and found out a few things about her. The moment I saw her walk in through the door, i went straight to talk to her and she was drawn by my candor, she said I was easy to talk to and very confident. we talked for hours unending, and it was one of the high points of my year.

2015 was over, and at the 2016 new year came, I wrote a list down, I asked God for three things that year. First of all, I wanted to graduate from my masters program that year, I wanted a job when I finished school and I wanted a life partner. Things weren't as clear to me at first, but the day I wrecked my car and needed assistance, she was there for me, through all the stress of not having a car in Houston, then it hit me, God had answered all my prayer points i listed at the beginning of the year. Kelsey is the love of my life, and forever is not enough time, but I will relish each second loving her and being her husband.
